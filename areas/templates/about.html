About Us

Behind the scenese of Safe Explorer

Founder's Mission

Safe Explorer was created by Izabela Markiewicz, a Business Information Systems student at University College Cork, as part of her Final Year Project. An avid traveller, Izabela was inspired to create Safe Explorer after experiencing her own share of unsettling encounters while travelling. Izabela's goal is to promote safe travel and to provide users with a tool that can help them make informed decisions about their safety and security.

CSO Crime Data

Safe Explorer uses official crime data from the Central Statistics Office (CSO) to provide users with accurate and up-to-date information about crime rates in different areas. The CSO is the national statistical office of Ireland and is responsible for collecting, compiling, and disseminating a wide range of statistics about the country. The data collected by the CSO is used to inform government policy and to provide the public with information about the state of the nation. Safe Explorer uses this data to provide users with information about crime rates in different areas, allowing them to make informed decisions about their safety and security.

Moderated Reviews

To maintain the integrity of the platform, all user reviews are moderated by the Safe Explorer team. All reviews are checked for accuracy and appropriateness before being published on the site. It is our aim to encourage tourism, although we are aware that some businesses may be affected by negative reviews. If you have any concerns about this, please contact us.
